Patrols To ensure a safe and secure campus environment, Concordia’s Security Department patrols the premises of both Sir George Williams and Loyola campuses on an ongoin basis, both on foot or and in a patrol car. Security agents are professionals who are trained to deal with emergencies and are available to answer your questions or provide support. They play a key role in making our campuses safe. Do not hesitate to seek their advice or support. Safe Walk A Safe Walk Program, also commonly referred to as accompaniment services, is available to members of the Concordia community, 24 hours a day. You can call Security at (514) 848-3717 to request an agent to accompany you to your vehicle, public transport route, or on-campus location. This service is subject to assessment and certain restrictions. If you feel you are being pursued, harassed or threatened by one or several individuals, on campus, please call Security immediately: Internal (university) phone: dial 3717, press 1 External phone: 514-848-3717 option 1 We encourage people to call Concordia’s Security Department first because our security agents are familiar with our campuses. However, if you have called 911 , please call Concordia’s Security Department so they can help facilitate the response.   Access Cards Concordia employees and authorized students may obtain an access card for designated spaces on campus. To do this, you must complete a form through your department, and obtain approval by your department. Please allow at least five business days for your request to be processed. Forms are available from the Security Department.   SGW Campus Send forms to Key Control (GM-1100) to be verified and date stamped. It is then sent to the Security Department for processing. You will be notified when to visit the EV Security Desk to have your photo taken and access card printed. Make sure you bring your valid Concordia I.D. card. For more information, please contact sgw.accesscard@concordia.ca . Loyola Campus Departments can send requests to SP-170 for verification and processing. Once processed, you can visit the Security Desk at SP-144 to have your photo taken and access card printed. Make sure you bring your valid Concordia I.D. card. For more information, please contact: vidya.khan@concordia.ca   Monday to Friday, 07:30 - 21:00 SGW Campus: EV 1.402 Security Desk Loyola Campus: SP 144 Security Desk If you lose your access card, please inform Security immediately by calling 514-848-3717 (option 2) or by emailing security@concordia.ca .   After Hours Access Authorized personnel (faculty, graduate students, staff) may be admitted, after hours, to their normal place of work. All other individuals who want access to university premises after hours must obtain authorization from the appropriate department or unit. The names of these individuals, along with all other relevant information, must be forwarded to the Security Department at security@concordia.ca , as per Concordia’s Policy on Working Outside the Hours of Operation or in Isolation (VPS-6) .   Room Access To access your office, a booked classroom, or auditorium on campus, please call Security at 514 848-3717 (option 2). A security agent will be dispatched to your location. You may be asked to present your university ID and/or proof of your reservation.   Keys Key services are coordinated by Facilities Management. Please call 514-848-2424, ext. 2400 for more information. Please immediately report the loss of a university key to Security by calling 514-848-3717 (option 2) or by emailing security@concordia.ca .
